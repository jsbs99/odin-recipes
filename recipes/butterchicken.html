Butter Chicken

Description:

Butter chicken is a very popular, and very delicious curry. With juicy, flavorful chicken and an incredibly creamy curry sauce, this recipe is sure to please almost anyone. The best part- it is very simple to make!

Ingredients:

For the chicken:

  • 1 Pound chicken breast/thigh
  • 1/2 tbsp Garlic paste
  • 1/2 tbsp Ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp Kashmiri chili powder

For the curry:

  • 500g (1lb) Coarsely chopped tomatoes
  • 100g (1/3ish) Onion
  • 50g (1.75 oz) Cashews(Preferrably unsalted)
  • 1 tbsp Garlic paste
  • 1/2- 2 tsp Garam masala (I like more)
  • 3 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Kashmiri chili powder
  • 5 tbsp butter, divided
  • 2 tbsp Malt OR 1.5 tbsp White vinegar
  • 3 tbsp Cream
  • 1 tsp Kasoori methi (Fenugreek)
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Chop the chicken into small/medium pieces and mix with garlic/ginger paste and chili powder
  2. Marinate at least 20 mins (Preferrably all day/ overnight if possible)
  3. Cook chicken in pot/saucepan over medium heat with oil
  4. Transfer cooked chicken to a plate or bowl for later. Do not rinse the pan
  5. Add chopped onion, a splash of oil, and 1 tbsp butter to pot. Sweat onions
  6. Once onions are ready, add tomatoes, cashews, garlic paste, sugar, garam masala, chili powder, vinegar, some water, and salt to taste
  7. Stir and let mixture simmer for 20 minutes
  8. Puree the mixture in blender. Be sure to vent the blender frequently, as the mix is hot
  9. Strain mixture back into original pot using a large spoon to push liquid through. Discard any remaining solids
  10. Add butter, cream, chicken, and kasoori methi. Let simmer for 5-7 minutes
  11. Serve with basmati rice!
