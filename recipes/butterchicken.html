Butter Chicken

Description:

Butter chicken is a very popular, and very delicious curry. With juicy, flavorful chicken and an incredibly creamy curry sauce, this recipe is sure to please almost anyone. The best part- it is very simple to make!

Ingredients:

For the chicken:

  • 1 Pound chicken breast/thigh
  • 1/2 tbsp Garlic paste
  • 1/2 tbsp Ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp Kashmiri chili powder

For the curry:

  • 500g (1lb) Coarsely chopped tomatoes
  • 100g (1/3ish) Onion
  • 50g (1.75 oz) Cashews(Preferrably unsalted)
  • 1 tbsp Garlic paste
  • 1/2- 2 tsp Garam masala (I like more)
  • 3 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Kashmiri chili powder
  • 5 tbsp butter, divided
  • 2 tbsp Malt OR 1.5 tbsp White vinegar
  • 3 tbsp Cream
  • Salt to taste
