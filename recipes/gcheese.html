Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description:

The grilled cheese that we know and love today is an American classic. It doesnt get much simpler than two pieces of bread and a slice of cheese. However, there is no other food that is this easy while also being this delicious. This version of the classic grilled cheese uses pepper jack cheese and a ludicrous amount of butter. Follow along and prepare to have your mind blown by how delicous one simple sandwich can be.

Ingredients:

Makes one sandwich

  • 1-2 Slices pepper jack cheese
  • 2 Slices your favorite white bread
  • A lot of butter (usually around 1/4 stick)

Steps:

  1. Place pan on burner on low/medium heat
  2. While pan is warming up, assemble sandwich
    • Place 1 slice of bread on plate
    • Place pepper jack on bread, making sure all the surface is covered
    • Place top slice on the first two layers
    • Voila!, A cheese sandwich
  3. Add butter (~1 tbsp) to pan, place sandwich on top after a few seconds of melting
  4. Let the sandwich grill for a few minutes
  5. Flip the sandwich over while adding another tbsp of butter to the pan
  6. Once both sides are golden brown, remove the sandwich from the pan, slice in half, and enjoy!
  7. Repeat until you run out of cheese or bread, whichever comes first
