Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description:

The grilled cheese that we know and love today is an American classic. It doesnt get much simpler than two pieces of bread and a slice of cheese. However, there is no other food that is this easy while also being this delicious. This version of the classic grilled cheese uses pepper jack cheese and a ludicrous amount of butter. Follow along and prepare to have your mind blown by how delicous one simple sandwich can be.

Ingredients:

Makes one sandwich

  • 1-2 Slices pepper jack cheese
  • 2 Slices your favorite white bread
  • A lot of butter (usually around 1/4 stick)
